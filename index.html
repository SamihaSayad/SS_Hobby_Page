Jewelry Making in the Past

In the past jewelty making went through four stages. The first Known jewelry found was in africa. It was made from shells and ostrich eggs. Then it wasn't until 3000 BC in Ancient Egypt when they started to make gold jewelry.After that came the Roman jewelry. Beside the gold they used sapphiers, emeralds and amber. Then in the middle ages they started to put jewelry on the clothes and the rings were popular at that time. The last stage was in the 19th century when they started using precious stones and matching suites.
